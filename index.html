Campus Clicks is a creative photography-based game conducted within the JNTUGV college campus. This game encourages participants to explore their surroundings and capture the beauty, moments, and unique perspectives of campus life through photography.

All submitted photographs are reviewed by the organizers or a judging panel. The photos are evaluated based on creativity, clarity, composition, originality, and overall visual impact. After careful evaluation, the best photograph is selected.

ABOUT US

ECENTRA is a platform designed to enable students to showcase their technical talents and learn new concepts in an effective and engaging manner. It promotes knowledge sharing and collaborative learning by bringing together a large gathering of students from various institutions.

With a focus on emerging technologies, ECENTRA 2K26 is themed to attract students by organizing a variety of technical activities at a single venue, including workshops, technical events, paper presentations, and project expos. The symposium encourages continuous learning and innovation, evolving progressively year after year.
